name: Paintings | Elden Ring Wiki
PaintingsinElden RingareInfo Itemsscattered all around The Lands Between that act as a form of treasure hunt. TheWandering Artist Spirit'sPaintingsdepict specific locations you'll have to find in order to meet the spirits of the artists themselves. Upon finding and inspecting one of the seven drawings, a ghost of theartistwill manifest at the location from which they were painted, granting a specific reward. Note that the location you must seek is not the structure or landscape depicted itself, but rather the spot the artist would have been when painting it. This page covers a full list of all thePaintingsfound in Elden Ring, including their location, solution, and rewards.
PaintingsareInfo Itemsthat can be found scattered across the world ofELDEN RING. Once theTarnishedhas discovered and interacted with the artwork, they're then able to perceive the soul of that painting's creator and retrieve unique items from their location. The location of the soul is the location of the perspective depicted by the artwork itself.
The soul of painters are initially invisible when viewed from a distance but will eventually reveal themselves as the player draws nearer to them. Successfully locating a painter's spirit will make it dissipate before leaving a unique item behind for the player to pick up; the item is usually thematically related to the artwork it is received from.

First drop is probably the trickiest. Roll forward into the cliff face (don't jump) from position shown on screenshot above. Drop height is just below lethal, so you'll lose some health.  Use theLongtail Cat Talismanto mitigate fall damage.  Performing a plunging attack as you fall should also help you land onto the branch safely.
Follow this branch down to the end and walk off at the corner between the rock wall and where the branch protrudes out of the cliff face to drop down to a lower branch.
While on this branch, walk towards the rock face. Facing the cliff face, walk off into the corner to the left between the rock wall and where the branch connects with the cliff. to the top of column.
Walk along the branch until you are close to its tip. Turn 90 degrees to your left, then roll onto the broken column top
From here there is a much greater hop down to another branch.  Do this while mounted on Torrent. Make sure you are not holding any weapon that rests on your shoulder as that seems to cause double-jumps to fail.  Move fairly close to the lip of the broken masonary and jump diagonally into the rock wall, double-jumping part of way down onto the branch below.
While still Torrent, carefully and slowly follow the branch down, followed by a short jump down to a nearby pile of fallen rocks. The golem nearby would not react to you.
On approach the painter vanishes and leaves behind theAsh of War: Rain of Arrows.
Note: it is also possible to reach the painter using the spirit spring close to Fort Faroth. See the Ash of War: Rain of Arrows page for details.
The golem near the Rain of Arrows Ash of War, like all golem which use the halberd as a weapon, has a chance to  drop theGolem's Halberd.
