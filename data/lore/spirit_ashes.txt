Ashes, also calledSpirit AshesorAshen Remains, areitemsinELDEN RING(2022)that allow the player to summon spirits that can fight alongside them.
To summon spirits one must have theSpirit Calling Belland be within the radius of aRebirth Monument, then use the wanted ash directly from the inventory. Most ashes needFPorHPto summon, and you can only have one ash summoned at a time.
Each Ash can be upgraded to a maximum of +10 byRoderikausing eitherGrave Glovewort(Ashes and Warrior & Militiamen Ashes) orGhost Glovewort(Special Ashes and Puppets).
Upgrading a Spirit Ash improves itsHP,Stamina, flatDefense(though not the percentualDamage Negation), andAttack Powermultiplier.
They are all immune toBlood LossandFrostbite, all except theLand Squirt Ashesare immune toPoisonandScarlet Rot, about a quarter are immune toSleep, and theMimic Tear Ashesis the only Spirit Ash that isn't immune toMadnessandDeath Blight, in addition to its Sleep vulnerability.
Rebirth Monuments are small obelisks that appear across the land. They are needed to summon spirits ashes and their presence is notified in the screen with an icon. They are frequently found close to boss fights or dense clusters of enemies.
The Stormhawk King an ash that can't be summoned by the player. Instead, it's used to progress inNepheli'squest.

Spirit AshesinElden Ringare companions, often mimicking the Lands Between'sEnemies, summoned by theSpirit Calling Bellto aid you inCombat. This page covers how they function, the spirit ashes available, summon costs, and a community-driven list of their relative strength.
With the release ofShadow of the Erdtree,Spirit Asheshave received a new upgrade called Revered Spirit Ashes, which allows them to become more powerful even if they are already at +10 level. Do note that this only applies while you are on the Realm of Shadow.
With the release ofShadow of the Erdtree,20new powerfulSpirit Ashesare added to the already stacked64Spirit Ashes in the base game. This leads to a total of 84 Spirit Ashes available between the base game and the new content added with theShadow of the ErdtreeDLC.
Not only there are moreSpirit Ashesbeing added to the game with Shadow of the Erdtree, but also in the Realm of Shadow, players can use a new item calledRevered Spirit Ash, to make them more powerful. This new upgrade for Spirit Ashes allows them to exceed the power they have, even if they are already at+10level.
This page is up to date with all this new content. Players will be able to search and find all Shadow of the Erdtree New Spirit Ashes here, as well as to learn about their Stats, Attack Patterns, Requirements, Upgrade requirements, Best uses, and much more regarding the new Spirit Ashes added with the Shadow of the Erdtree DLC! This comprehensive guide will help players optimize their combat strategies utilizing Spirit Ashes.
NOTE: As of Patch 1.13.2 Multiple spirit ashes stats and stagger values were changed, along with Revered Spirit Ash scalings.
To useSpirit Ashes, players must first obtain theSpirit Calling BellfromRennaat theChurch of Elleh[Map Link].
Similar toItems, Spirit Ashes can be equipped to and used from your Quick Slots or Pouch. However, they can only be used while not inMultiplayer Coopand when near aRebirth Monument.
After summoning in a given area, you are not allowed to summon again in that area until you rest at anySites of Graceor Fast Travel.
Players may reuse the Spirit Ashes to dismiss a summon.
Leaving the boundary of a valid summoning area will dismiss any active spirits.
Spirits may only be summoned once per encounter, so you cannot re-summon them after they die during a boss fight.
Spirits cannot be summoned while the effects of theFurlcalling Finger Remedyare active. Use the remedy again to cancel its effects.
Summoning spirits require eitherFPorHPdepending on the summon.
Players must be near aRebirth Monument, stone obelisks found at many Points of Interest and nearly every boss arena, to use Spirit Ashes.
