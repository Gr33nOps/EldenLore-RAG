name: Walking Mausoleum | Elden Ring Wiki
Wandering Mausoleumsare specialLocationsinElden Ring. Mausoleums appear as walking buildings with four legs and are found throughout the Lands Between, often with their signature bells attached. Players must attack the skull-like deposits on the structure while avoiding damage to get the mausoleum to become accessible. These can appear on either the legs or along the base of the building, the latter of which necessitates jumping on top of it.
Players will hear mausoleums as they emit a distinctly loud bell sound as they walk about. They usually have a small roaming area, so they are easy to find. When you first want to access a wandering mausoleum, you must either attack the deposits on its feet or use the environment to climb up, jump onto the wandering mausoleum and destroy the deposits around the structure itself.  This can take considerable time depending on the location.
Once the mausoleum is static, you can enter it at any time to perform aRemembrance Duplication: This is only available once per mausoleum, so pick wisely! Performing this rite will enable you to obtain the multipleRemembrance Weapons (Boss Weapons)and magic that they grant. Boss Armor is not gated behind their remembrances, but you must have defeated them to purchase fromEniaat theRoundtable Hold.
There are a total of 7 Wandering Mausoleums in Elden Ring. Monuments with bells will duplicate any boss remembrance. Monumentswithoutbells will only duplicate the remembrance of a non-shard bearer boss. Each reward can only be received once per playthrough.
Liurnia of the Lakes, No Bell:North of theRuined Labyrinth Graceor, due south of theMausoleum Compound Gracein Eastern Liurnia, north of the Church of Vows. It stops wandering for about seven minutes during "late night".
Additionally, threeRemembrance Duplication Coffinswere added in the Shadow of the Erdtree expansion which serve the same purpose.

The negation numbers are the % of your damage that gets blocked. For example, if a negation is 60, 40% of that damage by that type will go through and 60% will be negated. Bigger number = less damage. A negation of 100 means no damage goes through, and a negation of -100 mean the enemy takes 2x damage from that source. 0 means damage goes pretty much as is.
The resistance numbers are the buildup amount to trigger it. For example, if a resistance is 100 you must deal 100 points of the given buildup to trigger it. Note that these go down over time, and increase each time the effect procs.
The Best Tips for Erdtree Burial Watchdog:
Kaiden Sellsword Ashesmake early Watchdog fights a breeze.
If you hit a Watchdog with severalCrystal Darts, it will go berserk and attack the closest thing, whether it be friend or foe. This can force it to focus on your summons while you attack it at range, but its most useful for forcing the Watchdog inImpaler's Catacombsto fight itsImpsor the Watchdogs inMinor Erdtree Catacombsto fight one another.
For melee users, the best way to win this fight is to bait its attacks. Try dodge rolling to its side after a strike, get a few hits in, then dodge away. Some of its attacks covers a wide range, so having a good shield is also handy, but avoid guarding against the Levitating attacks since they can hit over a shield. If you end up behind the Watchdog, beware of its Tail Slam attack that can still hit you.
Jump Attacks:Jumping is effective for dodging both the Levitating and Spinning Swipe attacks. Jump to dodge and then do a jumping attack to build upStancedamage and get incritical hits.
Parries:Since their attacks are well-telegraphed, they can beparriedwith relative ease.
This fight is considerably easier for ranged and magic users since all you need to do is keep your distance. You may have to roll or jump away several times during the Levitating attacks, since it likes to do this many times in a row, but eventually it will stop and switch to more easily punishable attacks.
Glintstone PebbleandLightning Spearare excellent spells for these fights. Spells that can stun them such asRock Slingcan also help.

Furnace Golem,焼炉のゴーレム(Shōro no Gōremu), is an enemy inElden Ring: Shadow of the Erdtree.
Furnace Golems are giant autonomous constructs made from metal branches and rods. They once spread a great inferno wherever they went, acting as weapons of mass destruction and instruments of unfathomable torture, gathering the countless, still-living bodies of man and beasts alike and added them to their ever growing collection of burning corpses. The charred human remains that are seen within are visibly locked in poses of terror and despair. Many of them reaching far outside the bars of their prison in a desperate, yet hopeless attempt to escape.
Their flames could burn away both body and soul,[1]and thus, on hismother'swishes, were used byMessmer the Impalerto cleanse the tower folk by fire,[2][3]in aCrusadewithout Grace or honor.[4]
As they were built specifically to mock and torment members of thehornsent culture, they are decorated with visages offire giantsand thefell god of firethat haunts their many sagas.[5]The mask depicting the fell god is derisively surrounded by curled horns, artifacts that which the hornsent revere most.[6]
The scattered sap of the Scadutree pools deep within the furnace golems, wherecrystal tearsare formed slowly over the ages.[7]Each Furnace Golem defeated will drop a tear that can be mixed in theFlask of Wondrous Physick.
The most common type of Furnace Golem that can be found throughout therealm of shadow. Its exposed legs allows one to deal damage to it with conventional melee attacks. All furnace golems are vulnerable toHefty Fire PotsandHefty Furnace Potsif the pots are thrown into the opening at the top of the golem, the latter of which is the more damaging variant of the two.[8]
Two of these golems can be found in theCastle Watering Hole. Where one of them, in its complete dormancy, will be blocking the entrance to theRuins of Unte. To gain access to the ruins and eventually fell this golem, then the player needs to re-awaken it with aHefty Furnace Pot, thrown into its cold, flameless basket.
Furnace golem with armored leg-bracers. Conventional melee attacks will not work on them. Players must instead find high-ground near said golem and try to throw in eitherHefty Fire PotsorHefty Furnace Potsinto its basket in order to deal damage.
The tactic of engaging the golem head-on and break its legs with repeated melee attacks.
While hitting either of its legs, it's useful to be keep the other leg in view to better identify which attack is about to come out next, so that it may be avoided accordingly. Once both legs have been broken, the golem will begin to collapse and fall face-first down on the ground, where it will persist for a very long time. Allowing for several, risk-free additional attacks and a severely-damaging critical attack to it's face. While the basket-opening at the top of the construct is easily accessible from ground-level while its knocked over, the furnace golem has near complete immunity to damage from either variant of hefty pots while in this state. However, this immunity ends immediately after performing a critical attack. So the player still has a chance to throw in a hefty pot for additional heavy damage before its able to stand up again.
Its shockwave attacks can be easily dodged by jumping (especially with Torrent's double-jump). If the golem is about to use its eruption attack, then its wise to run away and gain distance withTorrentand then get back into melee range again once the attack finishes. Do keep note that gaining too much distance for too long may trigger the golem to use its eruption attack again.
The tactic of locating nearby high-ground so that one may throw several hefty pots into the golem from above.
While only the armored variants force the player to use this method, this tactic is still applicable to every single furnace golem that can be found and is the fastest and easiest way to take out furnace golems bar none, as it does not require any stat-investment or weapon upgrades of any kind. The only things needed, are efficient resource-gathering for the required crafting materials and passable spacial awareness.
Note that it'll take about five to sevenHefty Fire Potsor two to fourHefty Furnace Potsto defeat furnace golems with this method.
