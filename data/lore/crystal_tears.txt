Crystal TearsinElden RingareKey Itemsthat can be mixed in theFlask of Wondrous Physickfor various special effects andbuffs. This page covers their names, effects, durations, andLocations. These for the most part stack with additional buffs that can be applied to your character, and you are limited to only twoCrystal Tearsthat can be put in your flask.
Crystal Tears, and theFlask of Wondrous Physick, are kept inNew Game+. They are also unique, meaning that they cannot be found in subsequent Journeys.
Shadow of the Erdtreeadds 8 newCrystal Tears, all of which can only be acquired from defeatingFurnace Golems.
TwoCrystal Tearscan be mixed into the flask at a time when visitingSites of Grace. Its use will be replenished after resting at a site of grace.
There are no limitations to which twoCrystal Tearsare combined.
There are a total of40Crystal Tearsthat can be found ingame.
Of the availableCrystal Tears, a select few can be found in pairs:
Crimson Crystal Tear- Together completely restoresHP
Cerulean Crystal Tear- Together completely restoresFP
Ruptured Crystal Tear- Together amplifies the damage of resulting explosion

Crystal TearsareKey Itemsused to apply temporary buffs inELDEN RING(2022). When resting at aSite of Grace, up to two Crystal Tears can be mixed into theFlask of Wondrous Physickto bestow temporary buffs when consuming the flask. The effect and duration of these buffs depend on the Crystal Tears used. The flask can only be used once, after which it can be replenished by resting at a Site of Grace.
Crystal Tears are often dropped by minibosses nearMinor Erdtrees(especiallyErdtree Avatars), found in stone receptacles across theLands Between, or dropped byFurnace Golemsin theRealm of Shadow.
